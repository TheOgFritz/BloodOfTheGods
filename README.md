Blood Of The Gods Mod
=== 
2 Custom items in the mod so far: Blood and Blood Bench. You can now find them in the creative menu under the Blood Of The Gods tab.

What's To Come?
===
Expect Bosses, unique custom boss arenas, custom swords with different powers, and more!

Blocks And Items, And What They Do: 
=== 
More about these items can be found on the [wiki](https://blood-of-the-gods.fandom.com/wiki/Blood_Of_The_Gods_Wiki)       
       
Blood Bench (block): fill the Blood Bench with blood to imbue your sword with blood (right click filled Blood Bench)       
        
Blood (Item): The Blood item can be obtained from killing bosses (there are none at this current moment). Use the Blood item on the Blood Bench (right click) to fill it with Blood.
